【English Version】
Welcome！！！Welcome to my Blog ~

I am currently pursuing a research-oriented master’s degree in Applied Economics at ISBF, Sun Yat-sen University. I’m truly fortunate to be a student of Professor Li Teng, under whose guidance I have embarked on my academic journey.

Although my research interests are not yet fully settled, I’m currently deeply fascinated by labor economics, health economics, urban economics, and development economics.

During my undergraduate studies, I majored in Finance. Here are some of the economics-related courses I’ve taken:

- Microeconomics (Samuelson, 88), Intermediate Microeconomics (Varian, 92) , Advanced Microeconomics (MWG, 98)
- Macroeconomics (Western Economics, 93), Intermediate Macroeconomics (Dornbusch, 90), Advanced Macroeconomics (Romer, 97)
- Econometrics (Stock & Watson, 95), Econometrics II (94), Social Survey and Data Analysis (based on Chen Qiang’s *Econometrics and Stata Application*, 95), Advanced Econometrics (97)
- Development Economics (94), Urban Economics (90), International Finance (95)  

I have also taken several mathematics courses. While my performance in first-year Calculus was average, I later took **Mathematics Analysis II** offered by the School of Mathematics during my senior year. Other courses include **Linear Algebra**, Probability and **Mathematical Statistics**, and **Business Statistics**.

Currently, I’m taking advanced courses in Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, and Econometrics. I’m committed to pushing myself and growing through the process.

I run a WeChat public account where I often share content about recent research papers, Stata, Zotero, LaTeX, and Markdown (though updates can be irregular).
